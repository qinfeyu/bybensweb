Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Home

ByBen's Supplements is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

When you place an order, we collect your first name, last name, phone number, delivery address, wilaya, and commune. When you contact us via the contact form, we collect your name and email or phone number, along with your message.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to process and deliver your orders, respond to your messages, and improve our service. We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Payment Information

ByBen's operates on a cash on delivery basis only. We do not collect, store, or process any payment card or banking information.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to save your shopping cart and language preference. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for secure database storage, Cloudinary for product image hosting, and Vercel for website hosting. Each service operates under its own privacy policy.

Data Retention

Order information is retained as long as necessary for business operations and legal obligations. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us via WhatsApp or Instagram.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, reach us on Instagram @bens.supplements or WhatsApp +213 662 269 449.
